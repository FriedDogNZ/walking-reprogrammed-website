Wairere Falls

Kaimai Mamaku Conservation Park, New Zealand

About this Walk

Wairere Falls is a waterfall with a beautiful walk-through forest greenery. The walk through the forest is an enjoyable process as you take in the sights of the trees and the grass. Along the way there is a bridge that gives you an early view of the falls showcasing a river connected to the waterfall. The river is a wonderful sight to see with plenty of shade around making it a good place to walk during a hot summer day.
