Ngarunui Beach

Raglan, New Zealand

About this Walk

Ngaranui beach walk is a relaxing walk on a beach at low tide. In this walk we have mt Karioi in the background which slowly gets closer the further you walk. Along the beach are calming and soothing puddles of water that you can walk through. The ocean is also visible at some points in the background reflecting the light from the sun making the water sparkle.
